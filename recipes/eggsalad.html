Egg Salad

Simply Eggcelent!

This simple yet amazing Egg salad recipe will have you craving more! It has only a handful of ingredients and is easy to hip up

The salad everyone will love and makes a perfect Sandwhich filling

Ingredients

  • 10 eggs
  • Mayonaise (keepiw if possible)
  • Chives
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. First 30 minutes before you boil the eggs, take them out of the fridge so they aren't cold anylonger. poke a hole in the top off the eggs with a pin needle. Put a large pot on the stove and bring watering to a rolling boil.

  2. Add the eggs to the boiling water and set a timer for 10 minutes. If the water stopped boiling then add a minute to the timer. After the timer runs out put the eggs in a bowl with icecold water. Once they have cooled peel them and then take an egg cutter and cut them length wise, turn them 90 degrees in the cutter and then cut them once more to cut dices.

  3. to the eggs add 1/4th of a cup of mayonaise, 2 teaspoons mustard, chives and salt and pepper. Mix and keep testing and adding salt and pepper until you like the taste.

And thats pretty much it! So enjoy!
