Cacio e Pepe

Super Fast, Super easy!

This pasta is my favorite if I dont have much time but a big hunger. Its cheese, salty, peppery and loaded with those carbs that just comfort you sometimes after a stressy week!

With 3 Ingredients, you cant go wrong and you will love this superfast pasta dish!

Ingredients

  • 400 gr. Spaghettie
  • 250 gr. Pecorino
  • Pepper (ground) and Salt

Instructions

  1. In a large pot bring salted Water to a rolling boil. Now be gentle with the salt the Pecorino is salty. Cook the pasta al dente by following package instructions.

  2. When the Pasta is 2 minutes away from being done, put a metal bowl on the pot with the pasta to heat it.

  3. Before you pour the pasta, take away some of the pasta water for the sauce. Strain the pasta and put it into the heated bowl.

  4. Add the pecorino and some of the hot pasta water and mix until all is incroporated to a creamy sauce.

  5. Now grind some fresh Black pepper over it and serve.

And Voila! Thje Pasta is done. Enjoy!
